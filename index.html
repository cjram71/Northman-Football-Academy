Frequently asked questions

What players and parents need to know.

The Academy is different from the open Northman camp format. It is a continuous development program for a narrower age group and requires an ongoing commitment.

For general Northman camps, showcases and current event listings, visit Northman Football Camps.

Does the Academy replace my local club?

No. Players must remain active members of their local club and attend club practices and games. The Academy supplements local football with additional development, coaching and exposure.

Who can apply?

Dedicated European American football players aged 14–17 who belong to a local club and are prepared to follow the monthly training, camp and digital-learning structure.

What is included in the €50 monthly fee?

The planned membership includes monthly academy camps, individualized strength and speed programming, digital team meetings, film review, US coach sessions and access to Northman events and showcases.

Are travel and accommodation included?

No. Families should expect to arrange their own travel and accommodation for rotating academy weekends unless a specific event announcement states otherwise.

Does membership guarantee US recruitment or a scholarship?

No legitimate program can guarantee an offer or scholarship. Northman helps athletes develop, prepare credible recruiting materials, attend showcases and connect with coaches who evaluate international talent.

How are position groups managed?

Roster spots should be limited by position so quarterbacks, receivers, linemen and defensive groups maintain useful player-to-coach ratios. Applications may be waitlisted when a position group is full.
